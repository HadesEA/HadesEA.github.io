About me

I am a professional with a strong technical and creative orientation, specialized in developing web applications and digital solutions using technologies such as Django, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design tools like Bootstrap. My experience covers everything from designing and structuring web projects to implementing dynamic charts, integrating databases, and creating user-centered interfaces.

Throughout my projects, I have demonstrated skills in identifying functional and non-functional requirements, as well as in the detailed diagnosis and documentation of technical issues. My approach combines rigorous data analysis from official sources with a continuous search for efficiency, sustainability, and usability in the solutions I develop.

I stand out for my self-taught mindset and initiative to solve complex problems, adapting tools and methodologies to the specific needs of each project. Additionally, I maintain a constant interest in improving code quality, visual structure, and the presentation of results, always ensuring that applications are scalable, maintainable, and accessible for end users.
